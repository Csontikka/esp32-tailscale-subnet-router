SIGNAL BARS ANATOMY (.signal-bars):
  4 bars, heights 25%/50%/75%/100% of 14px container
  Active bars colored by quality class:
    on-excellent: --success  (≥ -55 dBm)
    on-good:      --success  (≥ -65 dBm, 80% opacity)
    on-fair:      --warning  (≥ -75 dBm)
    on-weak:      --warning  (≥ -85 dBm, 70% opacity)
    on-poor:      --danger   (< -85 dBm)
  Inactive bars: --border color

RESET REASON BADGE COLORS:
  POWER_ON   → badge-neutral  (grey)
  SW_RESET   → badge-neutral  (grey)
  PANIC      → badge-danger   (red)  + crash-info card shown below
  WDT        → badge-warning  (amber)+ crash-info card shown below
  BROWNOUT   → badge-warning  (amber)
  FLASH/OTA  → badge-info     (cyan)

HEAP METRIC THRESHOLDS:
  ≥ 100 KB → text-success + success bar + card-stripe-success
  50–99 KB → text-warning + warning bar + card-stripe-warning
  < 50 KB  → text-danger  + danger  bar + card-stripe-danger
